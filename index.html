Hi there!

My name is Harry Zhang. You may call me Haochen. Welcome to my personal website, and I hope you enjoy the experience as much as I did when designing it.

I was born in Beijing, China and came to Virginia Beach, the United States at age 14. Currently, I am attending Emory University in Atlanta, GA majoring in Sociology and Business (Strategy & Management Consulting and Finance).



Before you begin further exploring my world, I want to share with you some secrets of mine.



    My parents taught me to always be honest and genuine.

    My best friends cautioned me to maintain work-life balance and enjoy life to the fullest.

    My mentors encouraged me to pursue interests and help others in need.




I love them, so I listened to them carefully.

In this website, I also want to be “honest and genuine” with you, no matter whether you are family, friend, acquittance, or a stranger to me. My life is colorful, so you will see more than just my happiest memories.

To echo my best friends’ concerns, I have divided the main content of the website into “life” and “work”. You will be given cliff note versions of my work and life experience.

I have always been an avid learner driven by curiosity, which can be reflected by my heavy course-loads since before elementary school. Some of my interests and corresponding stories are shared and elaborated in the “life” section. On another note, coffee is my favorite beverage, and I am more than happy to chat over coffee on any intriguing subjects. Feel free to contact me through the provided channels.

sincerely,
